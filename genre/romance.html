Romance Movies

Grab your someone, a blanket and be prepared to get teary-eyed because here are the top ten romance movies of all time!

#1. Forrest Gump

Rating: 8.8

The presidencies of Kennedy and Johnson, the Vietnam War, the Watergate scandal and other historical events unfold from the perspective of an Alabama man with an IQ of 75, whose only desire is to be reunited with his childhood sweetheart. Watch the preview here!

#4. Good Will Hunting

Rating: 8.3

Will Hunting, a janitor at M.I.T., has a gift for mathematics, but needs help from a psychologist to find direction in his life. Watch the preview here!

#7. Silver Linings Playbook

Rating: 7.7

After a stint in a mental institution, former teacher Pat Solitano moves back in with his parents and tries to reconcile with his ex-wife. Things get more challenging when Pat meets Tiffany, a mysterious girl with problems of her own. Watch the preview here!

#10. The Curious Case of Benjamin Button

Rating: 7.8

Tells the story of Benjamin Button, a man who starts aging backwards with consequences. Watch the preview here!

#2. Titanic

Rating: 7.9

A seventeen-year-old aristocrat falls in love with a kind but poor artist aboard the luxurious, ill-fated R.M.S. Titanic. Watch the preview here!

#5. Slumdog Millionaire

Rating: 8.0

A Mumbai teenager reflects on his life after being accused of cheating on the Indian version of "Who Wants to be a Millionaire?". Watch the preview here!

#8. Life Is Beautiful

Rating: 8.6

When an open-minded Jewish waiter and his son become victims of the Holocaust, he uses a perfect mixture of will, humor, and imagination to protect his son from the dangers around their camp. Watch the preview here!

#3. Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

Rating: 8.3

When their relationship turns sour, a couple undergoes a medical procedure to have each other erased from their memories. Watch the preview here!

#6. Amélie

Rating: 8.3

Amélie is an innocent and naive girl in Paris with her own sense of justice. She decides to help those around her and, along the way, discovers love. Watch the preview here!

#9. Shrek

Rating: 7.9

A mean lord exiles fairytale creatures to the swamp of a grumpy ogre, who must go on a quest and rescue a princess for the lord in order to get his land back. Watch the preview here!
